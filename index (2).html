Welcome to the website for the game City Drive-beside the road. Here you can download the game and drive your car around the city.

About the Game

This game was made for fans of open world games. You can complete missions or simply drive around the city. Feeling bored? You can even get out of your car and walk around!

System Requirements

  • Processor: Intel Core i3
  • Graphics: Intel UHD Graphics
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Storage: 5 GB or more available space
  • Input: Keyboard, Mouse, Controller

Languages

City Drive-beside the road is available in the following languages:

  • English
  • German
  • French (coming soon)
